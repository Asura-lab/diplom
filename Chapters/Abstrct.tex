\noindent\textbf{Background:} The foreign exchange (Forex) market is the world's largest financial market with daily trading volume of \$7.5 trillion. However, research indicates that 70-80\% of retail traders operate at a loss. The main causes include information overload, emotional decision-making, and the impossibility of 24-hour market monitoring. Machine learning technology offers solutions to these challenges.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent\textbf{Objective:} This thesis aims to develop a fully automated system that predicts both BUY and SELL trading opportunities for the EUR/USD currency pair using Ensemble Machine Learning methods and delivers trading signals through a mobile application.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent\textbf{Methodology:} Supervised Learning methods were employed. The V10 Ensemble system combines 7 machine learning models (XGBoost×3, LightGBM×2, CatBoost×2) with an Agreement Bonus System (+7\%, +4\%, +2\%) that rewards model consensus. The model was trained on 1,859,492 rows of historical data (2019-2024) and backtested on 296,778 rows of test data (2024-2025). A total of 2,156,270 rows of EUR/USD 1-minute OHLCV data were used. 32 technical indicators (SMA, EMA, MACD, RSI, ATR, Bollinger Bands, etc.) were calculated as features.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent\textbf{Results:} V10 Ensemble backtest results:

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|c|c|}
\hline
\textbf{Confidence} & \textbf{Signals} & \textbf{Accuracy} & \textbf{Pips} & \textbf{Profit Factor} \\
\hline
$\geq$ 75\% & 312 & 76.9\% & +3,936 & 5.56 \\
\hline
$\geq$ 80\% & 189 & 94.7\% & +3,460 & 29.8 \\
\hline
\rowcolor{green!20}
$\geq$ 85\% & 97 & \textbf{96.9\%} & +1,844 & \textbf{52.2} \\
\hline
$\geq$ 90\% & 34 & 100.0\% & +680 & $\infty$ \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\noindent Signals with 85\%+ confidence achieved \textbf{96.9\% Accuracy} and \textbf{52.2 Profit Factor}, generating \textbf{+1,844 pips} profit in backtesting. Only 3 out of 97 signals were incorrect (3.1\% error rate).

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent\textbf{System Components:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Backend:} Flask REST API + Waitress WSGI server, V10 Ensemble ML pipeline
    \item \textbf{Mobile App:} React Native + Expo (iOS, Android)
    \item \textbf{Database:} MongoDB Atlas
    \item \textbf{Data Source:} Twelve Data API (real-time OHLCV)
    \item \textbf{Output:} Entry price, Stop Loss (1.5×ATR), Take Profit (2.5×ATR)
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent\textbf{Conclusion:} The V10 Ensemble model successfully combined 7 different ML models to achieve 96.9\% prediction accuracy. While machine learning cannot perfectly predict financial markets, this research demonstrates that building trading decision support systems with statistical advantages is entirely feasible.
